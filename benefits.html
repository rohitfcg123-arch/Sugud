Why choose Sugud (Jaggery Powder)?

Sugud is a healthier alternative to refined sugar. Mined from natural cane (or palm), it retains minerals and a rich flavor that refined sugar lacks. Below are the key benefits that make Sugud an ideal choice for everyday use.

Less Processed

Made with minimal processing — preserves natural nutrients and flavour.

Rich in Minerals

Contains iron, calcium, magnesium and potassium — small amounts that refined sugar lacks.

Better Taste Profile

Caramel-like notes add depth to beverages, desserts and recipes.

Energy Boost

Provides quick, natural energy — good for pre-workout snacks and traditional remedies.

Traditional & Versatile

Used in many cultural recipes, Ayurvedic remedies and sweets — versatile across cuisines.

Supports Local Producers

Choosing Sugud helps small-scale farmers and traditional producers.
