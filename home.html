FAQ

Is Sugud organic?

We prioritise clean processing and quality testing. Organic certification can be added; contact us for the latest status.

Can I use it in tea and coffee?

Yes. Sugud is fine and free-flowing, so it mixes well. Start 1:1 with your usual sugar spoon and adjust.

Does it contain sulphur?

No sulphur is added in our process.

Shelf life & storage

Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. Use a dry spoon to avoid lumps.
