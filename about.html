About SUGED

SUGED is committed to offering natural, unrefined jaggery powder made from pure sugarcane juice using traditional methods. Our product is free from chemicals and preservatives. We believe in healthy sweetness — a simple swap that brings better nutrition to daily life.

Why choose SUGED?

  • 100% natural — no preservatives
  • Traditional processing for natural minerals
  • Convenient powdered form for everyday use

How to use

Use SUGED in tea, coffee, sweets, or when baking. For a healthier daily diet, substitute equal quantity of jaggery for sugar in most recipes.
