About Sugud

Our journey from traditional sweetness to modern kitchens.

Our Story

Sugud was founded with a simple mission — to bring back the natural sweetness of jaggery into every household. In a world dominated by refined sugar, we wanted to revive the healthier, more traditional option that our grandparents swore by.

We source sugarcane from trusted farmers and process it carefully without sulphur or chemicals. The result is pure jaggery powder — easy to use, rich in flavour, and packed with nutrients.

Our Mission

To replace refined sugar in kitchens with a healthier, natural alternative — without compromising on taste or convenience.

Our Values

  • 🌱 Purity & natural ingredients
  • 🤝 Fair trade with farmers
  • ✅ Consistency in quality
  • 🌍 Sustainable practices

Why Choose Sugud?

Unlike regular jaggery blocks that are hard to measure, Sugud offers finely powdered jaggery that dissolves easily. It’s convenient for tea, coffee, sweets, and baking. With Sugud, you get the richness of tradition with the ease of modern cooking.
